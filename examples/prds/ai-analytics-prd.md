# PRD: AI Content Analytics Launch

## Context
Marketing and content teams need faster reporting on article performance and content quality.

## Problem
Manual dashboard pulls delay editorial planning by 2-3 days.

## Goals
- Deliver weekly automated insight summaries.
- Detect low-performing topics early.
- Recommend optimization opportunities.

## Requirements
- Pull engagement metrics daily.
- Compute quality rubric trend over time.
- Generate plain-language weekly summaries.

## Success Metrics
- Reporting lead-time reduction
- Analyst hours saved
- Improvement in average engagement score
